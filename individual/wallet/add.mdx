## Overview

Apple Wallet and Google Wallet are both digital wallets pre-installed on your phone, it's the app where you also store your boarding passes and payments cards.

With Tapify you can now add your business card to it, for quick and seamless sharing of your contact details.

## Add to Wallet

1. **Activate your Business Card** – Follow the steps [here](https://docs.tapify.app/individual/wallet/apple).

2. **Download your card**:

   * Go to [Settings > Share](https://tapify.app/settings/share).

   * If on mobile, choose your Wallet (Apple or Google) to add it instantly.

   * If on desktop, scan the **QR code** displayed to add it to your Wallet.
