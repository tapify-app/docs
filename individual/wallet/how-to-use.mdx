## Overview

Apple and Google Wallets let you easily share your **Tapify Profile**, making it simple to save or exchange contact details with people you meet.

## Accessing your business card

### Apple Wallet&#x20;

1. Double-click the **side button** on your iPhone to open Apple Wallet.

2. Find your Apple Wallet Business Card among your passes.

### Google Wallet

1. Open the **Google Wallet app**.

2. Find your Google Wallet Business Card among your passes.

## Share

Let the other person scan your **QR code** placed on your digital business card to open your Tapify Profile instantly.
