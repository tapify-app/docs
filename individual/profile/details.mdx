## Overview

Your Tapify Profile is your **digital business card**, displaying contact details, social links, and personal info. Updates sync instantly, ensuring you always share current information.

To edit your Tapify Profile details, go to [Settings > Profile](https://tapify.app/settings).

## Edit personal information

To update your personal information, follow these steps:

1. Click the **Edit** button to change your personal information.

2. Update the relevant fields:

   * **Profile photo** — Upload an image and use the crop function for best results.

   * **Name** — Your full name as displayed on your profile.

   * **Job title** — Your professional role or designation.

   * **Department** — Your department or area of expertise.

   * **About title** — A heading for your “About” section.

   * **About** — A short description about yourself.

3. Click **Save** to apply changes.

## Contact details

You can add multiple contact details to your Tapify Profile:

1. Under Contact details, click **Add**.

2. Select the type of contact detail you want to add:

   * Phone number

   * Email address

   * Website

   * Location (e.g., office address)

3. (Optional) Add a title.

4. Click **Save**.

## Social media

Add your social links to Tapify Profile.

1. Under Social media, click **Add**.

2. Choose a platform (LinkedIn, Instagram, Twitter, etc.).

3. Enter your profile link.

4. (Optional) Add a title.

5. Click **Save**.

<br />

<Note>
  To delete any contact details or social media click the X button while hovering.
</Note>
