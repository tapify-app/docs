## Overview

Tapify allows you to customize your Tapify Profile’s appearance to match your personal or professional brand. Adjust themes, fonts, and colors to create a profile that aligns with your identity.

## Customize

To update your profile’s branding:

1. Go to [Branding > Tapify Profile](https://tapify.app/).

2. Customize the following options:

   * **Theme** — Choose between light or dark mode.

   * **Style** — Adjust the roundness of your profile picture, icons, and buttons.

   * **Font** — Select a font that suits your brand.

   * **Brand color** — Set the color for icons and buttons.

   * **Banner** — Upload a banner image.

     * *Recommended size: 1080 x 675px (8:5 ratio).*

3. Click **Publish** (top right) to apply changes.

Your Tapify Profile will instantly update to reflect the new branding.
