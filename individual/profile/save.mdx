## Overview

The **Save Contact** button allows others to instantly store your contact details on their phone with a single tap. This ensures seamless and efficient networking without the need for manual entry.

## How it works

1. When someone views your Tapify Profile, they can tap the **Save contact** button.

2. This will open their phone’s contacts app with your details pre-filled.

3. To ensure the contact is saved correctly, they should select **Create New Contact**.

<br />

<Note>
  The contact details saved using the **Save contact** button are based on the information in your Tapify Profile.
</Note>
