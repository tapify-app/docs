## Welcome to Tapify

Tapify makes it easy to create, customize, and share your **digital business card**. Follow these quick steps to get started.

## Onboarding Steps

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Complete your profile" defaultOpen="false" icon="user">
    Add your contact details, social media links, and upload your profile photo to personalize your digital business card.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Set up your branding" defaultOpen="false" icon="palette">
    Customize the look of your Tapify profile and Wallet QR code card with your brand elements.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Add to your Wallet" defaultOpen="false" icon="wallet">
    Add your personalized Wallet QR code to your Apple or Google Wallet for easy sharing.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
