## What is Tapify?

Tapify is a smart, scalable solution for creating and managing **digital business cards**. Designed for both individuals and teams, Tapify makes it easy to share contact details, maintain brand consistency, and keep information up to date at all times — all in one intuitive platform.

## Getting started

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Create your workspace" defaultOpen="false" icon="seedling">
    Everything in Tapify starts with your workspace. If you haven’t set one up yet, or want to learn more about how Tapify works for teams, [book a demo](https://tapify.app/contact) with us, and we’ll guide you through the setup.

    If you’re ready to get started, [sign up](https://tapify.app/signup), and choose **For my organization** to start your 14 days free trial
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Add your workspace logo" defaultOpen="false" icon="image">
    Your workspace logo helps team members recognize your brand when they receive an invite.

    Go to [**Settings > General**](https://tapify.app/settings) to upload your logo.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Set permissions" defaultOpen="false" icon="gear">
    Set these permissions before inviting your team members to ensure your workspace fits your organization’s needs.

    Navigate to [Settings > Permissions](https://tapify.app/settings/permissions) to manage your team members permissions to access their digital business card, learn more about permission**s.**
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Set up templates" defaultOpen="false" icon="grid-2-plus">
    To design your team’s digital business cards, go to [Templates](https://tapify.app/templates) and set up both **Tapify Profile** and **Wallet templates** for a consistent brand experience.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Add your team members" defaultOpen="false" icon="user-plus">
    To add your team to your workspace, go to [**Team Management**](https://tapify.app/team) and click **Add**. Enter their contact details and socials to set up their digital business cards.

    You can add team members individually or in bulk.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
