To delete a team member:

1. Go to your workspace's [Team management](https://tapify.app/).

2. Click the **three dots** next to the team member you want to delete.

3. Select **Delete** from the dropdown menu.

4. Click **Delete** to confirm.

<br />

<Note>
  When you delete a team member from your workspace, your subscription will automatically adjust on a pro-rata basis.
</Note>
