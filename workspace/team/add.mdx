## Individual

1. Navigate to your workspace's [Team management](https://tapify.app/).

2. Click on **Add** button at the top right of this page.

3. Click on **Individual** to open the Add team member modal.

4. Enter the details of the users and click on **Add**.

5. Now, the team member has been added to your Tapify Workspace.

## Bulk import

To streamline the process, you can choose to bulk import by using CSV file. This creates multiple team members in one go. Here's how:

1. Navigate to your workspace's [Team management](https://tapify.app).

2. Click on **Add** button at the top right of this page.

3. Click on **In bulk** to open the Bulk upload modal.

4. Click on **Download CSV file** to download the template.

5. Fill in the template with the personal information for each user (each row corresponds to one team member).

6. Upload the completed CSV file to the workspace. This will automatically create new team members based on the provided information.

7. Now, the team members has been added to your Tapify Workspace successfully.

<Note>
