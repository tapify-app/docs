## **Overview**

We recommend ensuring your workspace is fully set up before inviting team members for a seamless onboarding experience. See [Getting Started](https://docs.tapify.app/workspace/introduction#getting-started) for details.

### Individual

To invite a single team member:

1. Go to your workspace's [Team management](https://tapify.app/).

2. Click the **three dots** next to the team member you want to invite.

3. Select **Invite** from the dropdown menu.

4. Check if the right [permission](https://docs.tapify.app/workspace/settings/permissions) is set.

5. Click **Invite** to confirm.

### In bulk

To invite multiple team members at once:

1. Go to your workspace's [**Team management**](https://tapify.app).

2. Select the team members you want to invite by checking their boxes.

3. Click **Invite** right at the top, it indicates how many team members will be invited.

4. Check if the right [permission](https://docs.tapify.app/workspace/settings/permissions) is set.

5. Click **Invite** to confirm.

<br />

<Info>
  Emails will be always sent the the **account email address** of the user.
</Info>
